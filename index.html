I am a Research Analyst at NORC at the University of Chicago. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at the University of Georgia. I obtained a M.A. in Political Science and International Relations at the School of Public and International Affairs at the University of Georgia where I also finished my B.A. in International Relations and B.A. in Spanish.
I work on survey data analytics using aspects of statistics, data vizualization, programming, and machine learning. I have employed these skills and more on projects such as AP VoteCast and the COVID Impact Study. During my academic and professional career, I have worked on many projects combining survey analysis, linear modeling, time series analysis, etc. In addition to producing and contributing to open source software, I have gotten a number of grants and awards to develop R packages for data visualization and statistical analysis.
Interests. Linear Modeling, Time-Series Analysis, Multi-Level Models, Bayesian Analysis, Survey Analysis, R, Open-Source Software, Sports Analytics
